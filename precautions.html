Precautions Before a Cyclone: Do's and Don'tsbr

Here are some precautions of cyclone one should follow to stay prepared for the disaster -

1.Ensure good condition of your house so that it can withstand strong winds.
2.Trim down trees to make sure they do not fall over telephone or electricity lines during the storm.
3.Keep your mobile phone fully charged and other appliances like torches handy as there may be power cuts during a cyclone.
4.Store essential items like groceries and medicines in advance. It is vital to keep dry foods that are not perishable during emergencies.
5.Prepare an emergency first-aid kit.
6.Store water because the water supply may be disrupted during cyclones.

Apart from the above cyclone precautions, one should avoid some factors at any cost while preparing for a storm. They are as follows:

1.Do not leave any loose sharp objects lying around your property, as they can injure people during gusty winds.
2.It is vital to steer clear of demolished buildings during cyclones.
3.Do not keep dying and low-hanging branches of trees near your premises.
